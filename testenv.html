2.- BASIC FLIGHT OPERATIONS

2.1. All users of Delta Virtual Air Lines must in either case, fly under any callsign of any SKYTEAM Airlines.

2.2. Users registered in our VA, may choose to perform a flight under the following categories: REGULAR or CHARTER.

2.3. All flights, in all cases, can be done in ONLINE mode while connected to the IVAO and VATSIM network or in OFFLINE mode.

2.4. In any case, you should choose between the types of aircraft that the SKYTEAM Alliance possesses in its fleet.

2.5. All flights reported, will be reviewed through the DRMK Tracker System, smartCARS and IVAO Tracker, in order to check the quality of the flight and the veracity of the report.

2.6. Upon finishing a flight, it must be reported on our website.

2.7. Each user must report a minimum of 1 flight every 12 months . Otherwise, your account could no longer be in active status.

2.8. Flights will be performed under the following systems:

DRMK Tracker System.

SmartCARS.

2.9. Wherever possible, we recommend flights be done consecutively, linear and seamless , i.e. take off from the airport where the user has landed before.

2.10. Follow the Flight Operations manual once you enter to the pilot room.

Skyteam System

SkyTeam is an alliance of Airlines. It is the second biggest airline union, founded by four of the largest companies in the world (Aero Mexico, Air France, Delta Airlines and Korean Air), which serves more destinations and flights around the world. Alliance is in constant growth that today reaches the figure of 20 partner airlines from all parts of the world.

As a pilot of Delta Virtual Air Lines, you can be a SkyTeam pilot, the system developed allows you to fly with any of these 21 airlines from around the world, always with the intention of flying around the globe simulating the codes shares of Delta in real life. That is why the management team has also incorporated more than 31,000 routes of the SkyTeam alliance.

We are incorporated as the 21st airline of our virtual SkyTeam alliance to the airline ICARO Virtual, which has a long history in IVAO.

SmartCARS

The SmartCARS system is a flight tracking software made for Microsoft FSX, Lockheed Martin Prepar3d and X-Plane by Laminar Research. This software has been acquired by the administrators of the airline and offered at no cost to our pilots for recording, reporting and filing their flights.

SmartCARS has not been designed only with virtual airlines in mind. It is ensuring that the experience is incredible for the pilots, who are the ones who will fly with it. It has been harshly tested by the Delta VA management team to ensure that it meets all the expectations of our pilots.

It is a platform in continuous expansion with new features, raising the bar high of what an ACARS should be.

It easily resumes flight tracking after a critical error of the simulator.

It works with Charter Flights as if they were Regular Flights.

It includes the altitude control system, with the option of co-pilot or GPWS warnings, Land Proximity Warning System. It also contains notices or messages from the flight crew.

It also incorporates different packages to personalize the sounds with extensive documentation.

Delta RMK Tracker System

Delta RMK Tracker System, better known as DRMK, is one of the most preferred reporting systems by our pilots. Created by Delta Virtual Air Lines, it is particularly remarkable for its simplicity, ease of use and functionality.

DMRK Tracker system stores all the information about your flight, reports automatically, and sends it to the personal database of each pilot.
